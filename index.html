    • Projects

  • Chinese Spatial Language Understanding Evaluation benchmark (project no. 2020AAA0106701)

    1. Jun 2021 - now
    2. Project Work: (1) Single sentence judgement, for classifying Chinese sentences that contain spatial expressions as acceptable or unacceptable according to their normal or abnormal spatial semantics; (2) Error explanation and sentence pair judgement task, for judging whether a reason can explain an incorrect sentence with spatial semantic anomaly; (3) We have marked more detailed temporal and spatial semantic relations. The corpus size is more than 2 million Chinese characters.

  • Construction of Chinese Syntactically-Annotated Corpus in Multi-domain Texts

    1. Jun 2018 - Jan 2021
    2. Project Work:We presented an approach of annotated syntax chunk which based on phrase structures at the discourse level. And constructed a system of an extensible corpus which provides intermediate syntactic analysis in phrase structure formalism. Based on this system, we selected multi-domain discourse as the annotated data, characterized the intraconstituents and extra-constituents in sentences, described the skeleton phrase structure and predicate-argument structure.At the preliminary stage, about 4.15 million words and 80,000 sentences of data were labeled, which provided high-quality corpus data for subsequent extended tasks and derived tasks. I participated in the formulation of norms, the papers writing and the implementation of work and so on.

  • Preposition Structure Collocation Database (project no. 2018YFB1005105)

    1. Jun 2018 - Jan 2019
    2. Project Work:We constructed a high-quality preposition structure collocation database based on the large-scale corpus. First,based on previous studies and linguistic rules, we established prepositional classification and collocation. Then, we designed and implemented a knowledge acquisition rules of prepositional structure collocation from large data. Finally, we tested and analyzed the extracted results. The main purposes are to obtain high-quality Preposition Structure collocations by formal means and to provide data support for natural language processing and basic linguistic and applied research.

  • Semantic Role Bank funded by Graduate Innovation Fund Project (project no.19YCX119)

    1. Jan 2018 - Sep 2019
    2. Project Work:We proposed a semantic role classification system based on previous researches, extracted the arguments of a Chinese verb and constructed the semantic role bank of Chinese verbs. So far, the project has examined 5,260 verbs. With the identification method, the arguments of 2,685 verbs are assigned with 4,307 semantic roles.

  • Construction and Application of Chinese Language Block Database in the Background of Big Data (project no.16AYY007)

    1. Apr 2017 - Jun 2018
    2. Project Work: We constructed adverbial-verb collocation database through linguistic rules and retrieval rules, extracted and evaluated data.
